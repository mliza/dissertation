\section{Ionization Study\label{ch4:ionizationStudy}}
    This investigation compares the index of refraction between a $5$ and $11$
    species gas. The solver used for this investigation is
    \gls{LeMANS}, which is a reactive hypersonic flow
    code \cite{Scalabrin2007}. \TabRef{tab:ionizationTableStudy} shows
    the cases that were tested; the simulations described below use a
    isothermal BC, with the famous Park's $2T$ \cite{Park1989} model.

    \begin{table}[H]
        \centering
        \begin{tabular}{|c|c|c|c|c|c|}
            \hline
            Case \# & Geometry & \# of Species & AoA $\Units{{\circ}}$ & M
            $\Units{\,}$ & Alt $\Units{km}$ \\
            \hline \hline
            1 & flat plate & 5 & 10 & 15 & 30  \\
            \hline
            2 & flat plate & 11 & 10 & 15 & 30  \\
            \hline
        \end{tabular}
        \caption{Ionization table study\label{tab:ionizationTableStudy}}
    \end{table}
