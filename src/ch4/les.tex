\section{High Fidelity Flow Study\label{sec:lesStudy}}
\Cref{tab:lesStudy} summarizes the flow
conditions used in this investigation. Two cases are considered: a subsonic
case $(1L)$ and a supersonic case $(2L)$. The $1L$ case, described in
\Cref{tab:lesStudy}, was validated by Tumuklu \cite{Tumuklu2024} through
comparison of \gls{CFD} results with experimental data. The $2L$ case, also
listed in \Cref{tab:lesStudy}, has not been validated directly; however, the
solver has been verified at subsonic speeds \cite{Molina2017}. \\

The primary objective of this section is to develop \gls{HAOT} capabilities for
turbulent flows. Both the subsonic and supersonic cases are employed to
investigate \gls{AO} properties as functions of temperature and density.
